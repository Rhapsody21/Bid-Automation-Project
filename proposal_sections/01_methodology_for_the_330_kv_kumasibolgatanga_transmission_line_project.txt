### Methodology for the 330 kV Kumasi-Bolgatanga Transmission Line Project

This proposal outlines a comprehensive methodology to meet the consulting services requirements for the 330 kV Kumasi-Bolgatanga Transmission Line Project, as specified by the Ghana Grid Company (GRIDCo). The methodology is designed to ensure effective project management, engineering excellence, and rigorous construction supervision, adhering to the Quality and Cost-Based Selection (QCBS) criteria.
